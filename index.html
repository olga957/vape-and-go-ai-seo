01

Executive Summary

Vape & Go should not run a separate "AI SEO" programme. It should add an AI visibility, automation and measurement layer to the existing 90-day SEO programme, whose principles are already right: commercial pages first, ClickUp as source of truth, one URL per task, keyword mapping before optimisation, AI-assisted execution, human QA, post-publish monitoring. Google supports this — its generative features use the core Search index and it treats AEO/GEO as SEO, not a separate discipline. Confirmed

The headline the merge exposes

SE Ranking's UK prompt database reports 0 ChatGPT presence. But Ahrefs counts 309 ChatGPT citations and GA4 records 1,391 real ChatGPT sessions with 58 conversions in 3 months. The "0" is a coverage gap in one tool's configured prompts — not reality. No single AI tool is ground truth: triangulate, and configure your own prompt cohort.

The biggest opportunities

  1. Correct quality & compliance weaknesses on high-priority pages.
  2. Improve category, brand & product-cluster architecture.
  3. Finish crawlability, raw-HTML, canonical & structured-data work.
  4. Operationalise the AI agents already planned in ClickUp — without duplicates.
  5. Configure a fixed AI prompt benchmark so AI visibility is measurable.
  6. Use selective authority building, not a generic backlink campaign.

Key Takeaways

Not starting from zero

The ClickUp workspace already holds: build-agents-for-SOPs; AI-vs-organic reporting; homepage E-E-A-T/trust; author schema & About; breadcrumb/schema/canonical/CMS; internal linking; Lost Mary/SKE/Hayati optimisation; Cloudflare & bot-crawl; dashboard/reporting. The "build agents" task is broad, the AI-vs-organic reporting task is waiting/overdue, and the homepage "AI/LLM tuning" task contains unsupported ideas (entity-confidence score, blanket compliance). Rewrite & operationalise — don't duplicate. Confirmed (ClickUp)

The largest organic gap is not simply authority

DR 64 ≈ Vape UK's 65, above Superstore (62) & Totally Wicked (53) — yet far fewer ranking keywords and much less traffic. The gap: commercial page coverage · category/subcategory architecture · ranking distribution · keyword-to-page intent matching · internal-link concentration · content-quality consistency · technical implementation/indexability.

AI tools disagree — measure your own

SE Ranking's set shows almost all measured AI visibility from Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT/Gemini at 0 — a coverage gap, not proof of absence (Ahrefs/GA4 show real ChatGPT presence). The 2.85% SOV is a diagnostic, not yet a KPI. A Vape & Go-specific 75-prompt benchmark is the fix. Confirmed

Compliance & factual quality are immediate SEO issues

The live SKE page carries single-use "dispose" framing, an absolute "leakproof" claim, safety-by-reputation, and cessation/"beats a pack of 20" language; the Hayati page reportedly uses "Ban-Proof Certified" and conflates TPD with safety. There is no general "ban-proof certification"; compliance ≠ safety. P1 SEO + legal fixes. Confirmed on live SKE page

03

Market Landscape

Traditional search remains primary

Confirmed Statcounter put Google at 91.17% of UK search in May 2026 (Bing 6.22%), consistent with the ~90% range verified independently. Google remains the primary organic discovery channel. Verify your channel mix in GA4.

Statcounter's separate UK AI-chatbot panel (different denominator, not comparable to search share): ChatGPT 70.16%, Copilot 10.21%, Gemini 8.15%, Claude 5.94%, Perplexity 5.52% — mirroring your own referrals.

Where AI search is meaningful

  • Product/category comparisons.
  • Compatibility questions.
  • "Which product is suitable for…".
  • Legacy-disposable → reusable migration.
  • Feature summaries.
  • Troubleshooting & setup.
  • Brand-reputation / retailer-selection.
  • Long conversational queries (query fan-out).

Why "half of searches use AI" is unusable

Two 2026 studies put AI-Overview activation at 13.7% vs 51.5% — query mix & method swing it wildly. Track your own cohort. Confirmed

Ecommerce opportunity & constraints

Google says sharing ecommerce data & structure helps it find/parse products — Merchant Center feeds, product info, logical architecture & valid schema beat "AI-friendly" content. Constraints: adult-only/regulated context · single-use ban · SKU-level legal verification · rapid stock/flavour change · legacy terminology · JS/AJAX platform (content may be missing from raw HTML) · AI repeating inaccurate claims · external-channel promo limits. Note: vape is not broadly suppressed in AI — hundreds of citations exist; Gemini is the exception (9). Confirmed

  • One verified compatibility source; answer buyer questions with first-hand content; build collection pages for unserved clusters.

  • Concentrate logged contextual links on near-wins; flavour → parent; log source/target/anchor/date.

Layer 5 — Trust & E-E-A-T

  • Verified entity, named authors/reviewers, original photos, policies; measurable PR; track concrete trust improvements, not a score.

Layer 6 — UX/conversion, compliance, freshness & monitoring

  • Clear compatibility/stock/options on mobile; visible compliance; VPD content; track rankings/citations/traffic/outcomes separately.

8.5 · Interpretation rules

  • Same prompts/engines/locale/schedule across periods.
  • Report 4-week rolling averages.
  • Separate brand mentions from citations.
  • Review relevance & false positives.
  • Compare competitors only within the same set.
  • Don't call a vendor "avg position" a universal AI rank.
  • Annotate major changes.
  • Correlation ≠ causation.

8.6 · AI crawler policy — checklist

No blanket allow/block; separate search visibility from training.
Allow OAI-SearchBot for ChatGPT visibility; decide GPTBot separately; keep Googlebot.
Quantify load via Cloudflare/logs; verify UAs & IP ranges; rate-limit abuse, don't block all.
Recheck indexability & AI visibility after any robots.txt change.

Recommended competitor-analysis process (per P1 cluster)

Export ranking keywords & pages from Ahrefs, SE Ranking and — once units restored — Semrush.
Map competitor pages by intent (brand hub / device / pod-refill / flavour / comparison / guide / support).
Check parity — equivalent page, or multiple intents on one URL?
Compare raw HTML, headings, product coverage, compatibility data, schema, internal links, original media.
Record only commercially relevant gaps; reject those lacking stock, causing cannibalisation, or relying on prohibited/legacy products.
Turn approved findings into existing canonical URL tasks or new Draft Tasks — never a raw gap export as an auto content calendar.
